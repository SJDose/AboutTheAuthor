About The Author

Stephanie Dose

Stephanie Dose has lived in the country near Fairfax since she was born in 2001. She grew up with one older sister and five older brothers. She attended St. Pauls Lutheran School from kindergarten through eighth grade and she is now a junior at Minnesota Valley Lutheran High School. She works part time as a crew trainer at Mcdonalds. She is involved with Student Council, Choir, and Art Club. She is also taking an AP Art course while in High school and plans to study visual arts further in college.
